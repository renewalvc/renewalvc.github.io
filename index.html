Led by a founder who changed the law to fund the future.

Ryan Feit co-founded SeedInvest, the platform that changed early-stage startup financing in the United States. He was instrumental in the passage of the 2012 JOBS Act — rewriting 80-year-old securities laws so entrepreneurs could raise capital over the internet — then worked with the SEC, FINRA, the White House, and the Treasury on its implementation.

As CEO he scaled SeedInvest to 850,000+ investors and 500+ funded startups before selling to Circle — principal architect of USDC, the dollar digital currency behind $20T+ in transactions across 190+ countries. There he joined the Executive Leadership Team and co-founded Circle Ventures, which has funded 100+ companies.
